Lital Hagayan

Music Producer, Sound & Mix engineer
Web Developer - coming soon:)

Lital was born in 29th January 1989, in Israel. since a young age of her life, she was very musical. her first instrument was keyboards, on the age of 8. she began to play very first compare all others in the class, so she jumped right away to the second class of keyboards class. after one year or so, Lital wanted to proceed with playing the keyboard so she decided to study piano! she took private lessons with a private nice teacher named Lena. Unfortunately, Lital's parents couldn't afford these days to buy a real piano, what forces Lital to keep on practice piano's melodies on the regular keyboards, but that situation effected her very badly and after two years, Lital decided to stop taking lessons.

On the age of 12, Lital decided to learn to play on the guitar. since her older brother played the guitar and there was a guitar at her home, she bought a chords book and learned alone by herself (and the chords book) playing the guitar. Lital felt very connected with the guitar and the process went quite well for her. also in those years, the internet developed and Lital was able to learn how to read guitar tabs, so she always found a short term of time to connect the internet (which caught the regular phone line at home these days) and download fast all tabs of each song she was willing to learn to play on the guitar.

When Lital got to high school, she entered to a music class. she already learned to play on electric guitar and play solos of many popular bands such as Pink Floyd, Beatles, Metallica, Nirvana, Led Zeppelin, etc...she got very professional and enjoyed every music class she entered. also Music is the highest grade of her high school diploma.

In the same days, Lital tried also to entered computers class, but she couldn't make it since she felt like her destiny belongs to music only.

After Lital finished high school, she enters to army service, as a Teacher Soldier to deaf teenagers which needed help with preparation for matriculation. Lital learned the sign language, and that is how she was able to communicate with those teenagers.

During her military service, one of Lital's best friends,Adir, died in a kit surfing accident. The loss was traumatic and difficult for her. One of the nights during seven days of the grief, she dreamed of him and the dream led Lital to write and compose a song about him. When Lital played the song to Adir's parents, they were so excited and it was decided that they were going to look for a music producer and a recording studio to make this song happen.

After the music producer with whom the song was produced was found, Lital entered the studio with the producer and they started working. At this point in Lital's life, she realized what she wanted to do "when she grew up." Lital wanted to be a music producer herself, she wanted to learn how to record instruments herself, run audio recording software, know how to create good sound, know how to mix and, in fact, know how to do everything from scratch.

and so it was! Lital fulfilled her dream as a musician, and to date, has been working in the music industry for 14 years. Lital has worked with well-known artists from the top ranks of the Israeli music industry: Harel Moyal, Margol, Yishai Levy, Tzvika Pick, Zehava Ben, Yishai Rivo, Amir Banyon, Nasrin Kadri, Michal Shapira, Shlomo Bar, Itai Levy, Yasmin Levy and many more.

In 2019 Lital went through a difficult crisis, which caused her deep heartache, which led her to non-stop writing and composing 11 songs. This is how Little Singularity's first album was born, which will be released on August 8, 2023.

During Lital's musical career, Lital found herself completely by chance, entering a job at a small high-tech start-up, which developed a music application and was just looking for a musician and sound man. Luckily for Little, she was a good fit for the company and that's how Little was exposed to the high-tech world for the first time. After a few years as a project manager in the same company, Little worked in another company as a project manager, while starting to learn Python, Java Script, html and the rest is history :)

Lital is a rare example of a person who fulfilled her dream and also found her love for development. Lital is admittedly only at the beginning of her journey, but with perseverance and dedication like she has - the sky is the limit, and she will go far :)
