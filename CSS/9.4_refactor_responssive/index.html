Lital Hagayan Singularity

Lital Hagayan is an Israeli musician, singer, songwriter and music producer. Is a self-taught composer, producer, audio and mix engineer, guitar and bass guitar. Her influences and work have encompassed a diverse range of genres including progressive rock, shoegaze, alternative rock, psychedelia, dream pop and electronic. Lital's first album created from a deep urge to express and heel from a dark and devastating period in her life. The album reflects her coping with a tragedy of a lost.The album was written, composed, produced and mixed by Lital Hagayan.
