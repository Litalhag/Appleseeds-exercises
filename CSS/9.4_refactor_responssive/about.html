About Me

Lital born and raised in Hod Hasharon, Israel. From the age of 8 Lital began to learn to play keyboards and later on the piano. But at the age of 12 she fell in love with the guitar and the rest is history. Immediately after her military service, Lital started working in a recording studio as a sound and mixing engineer, over time she also musically produced singles and albums of all kind of musicians. After 14 years in the profession, Lital's first album was born.

With a delicate and melodic wash of guitars and powerful invoking lyrics. Lital sings with beautiful harmonies and raw passion that leaves you wanting for more. She reveals her soul with her heart on her sleeve. Lital Hagayan is hailing from Israel , who is a Shoegaze, Alternative musician that is a self-taught composer, producer, audio & mix engineer, multi instrumentalist.

Eternity

The first track of the album and the first single which released in January 2020. A melody track which describes the inability to accept reality and the situation in which the character is.

Miss You

The last track on the album, and the second single which released in February 2022. An ambient track with layers of voices and layers of guitars and synths that describes a deep longing.

Like I Remember You

Like I Remember You has released in March 2022. The Song was written with a heart full of pain while waiting for a loved one to be healed, and with a deep sincere feeling of hope, that never materialised.
